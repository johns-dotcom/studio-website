About

Built for creators

The Nest is a professional recording studio built from the ground up for artists, producers, and engineers who demand more from their creative environment.

Every detail — from the acoustic design to the analog signal chain to the marble producer's bar — was considered with one question in mind: does this make the work better?

Whether you're cutting vocals, building beats, mixing a record, or writing your next project, The Nest gives you a space that matches the seriousness of your craft.
